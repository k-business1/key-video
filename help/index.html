🔍 Searching
How do I search for a movie or show?▾
Type a title into the search bar at the top of the page. Results appear automatically as you type — no need to press Enter.
I typed the wrong spelling and got no results▾
KEYTUBE automatically corrects small typos and matches the closest title in our library, so keep typing — it usually finds what you meant.
Can I filter by category or country?▾
Yes — use the category menu on the main page (English, French, Indian, Chinese, Drama, Comedy, Cartoon, News, Song) to browse by type.
🎬 Watching
The video won't play or keeps buffering▾
Check your internet connection first. If the issue continues, try refreshing the page or switching to a lower video quality from the player settings.
How does the Comedy Shorts (swipe) player work?▾
Swipe up or down to move to the next clip, just like short-video apps. The next video loads automatically when the current one ends.
Can I download videos?▾
Where available, a download button appears on the movie's details page below the player.
👤 Account
How do I sign in?▾
Click the Sign In button on the homepage and enter your Gmail address to create or access your account.
Why was my account blocked?▾
Accounts are blocked for violating community guidelines (spam, abusive comments, etc). Contact support below if you believe this was a mistake.
⬆️ Uploading (Admin)
How do I add new movies to KEYTUBE?▾
Admin access is required. Open upload.html, sign in with the admin password, then fill in the movie details and upload the cover image and video file.
My upload says "Unauthorized"▾
This means you're not signed in as admin. Re-enter the admin password on the upload page to get a valid session token before saving.
Where can I manage existing content?▾
Use the Admin Panel (admin.html) — the Movies tab lets you edit or delete any title, and the Dashboard shows overall stats.
